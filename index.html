Instructions

You will be given a ramdom speed number at the start of the game. There are four cars below. By clicking on a car you will be add a specific amount of points/speed to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score/speed to the random speed number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each car is hidden from you until you click on it.
